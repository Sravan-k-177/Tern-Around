Tern-Around

About Us

A travel quest app for discovering landmark-led trips and nearby hidden spots.

What Tern-Around is

Tern-Around helps travelers search places, compare landmarks, and discover nearby underdog spots through small on-site quests. The goal is to make trip planning feel more personal, more local, and less generic.

Why we built it

The site combines travel discovery, account sign-in, saved places, and quest tracking in one place so users can move from inspiration to action without switching tools.
